Hi there!


On a crisp and vibrant long weekend in October of this year, my best friend Charlotte and I embarked on a life changing experience to the breathtaking and beautiful Canadian national parks of Jasper and Lake Louise in Alberta. As the crisp autumn air embraced us in the Canadian Rockies, we were greeted by awe-inspiring views, clear blue lakes, and the promise of an adventure of a lifetime. A few of the most enchanting moments captured during our travels are above, one being the ethereal sunrise in Jasper. Our campsite was at the foothills of the Rockies, this Jasper morning treated us to an amazing mix of colors as the sun rose, casting a beautiful orange hue over the mountains. The first photograph shows the calm beauty of the morning, with hues of pink and orange painting the sky like something i've never seen. The peacefullness of the scene reflects the serenity and awe we felt in those early moments, a perfect way to wake up and discuss the adventures awaiting us in the day ahead.

Another cherished memory captured in a selfie is the joy and happiness we felt during one of many perfect hikes in Jasper. I think the second photo radiates the joy as Charlotte and I stand against a backdrop of the rocks and lakeshore we were about to do a cold dive in. I can only hope our smiles capture a sliver of the sheer excitement of discovering hidden trails, exchanging stories, seeing wildlife, and reveling in the bond that our travels have strengthened. The photo serves as a touching reminder that the true meaning of a journey lies in more than just in the places you go, but also in the people you go with. The third photo shows a moment of personal triumph, as I found myself scaling a rather large rockie form during another wonderful hike in Jasper. With the trees surrounding me, I think the photo encapsulates our spirit of adventure and exploration. I tend to not move out of my comfort zone, and this climb was something that was very challenging for me. The pride I felt in myself when I reached the top and got to stare at the view of a lake and mountainous range in the back ground in awe was unimaginable. In this moment, I was moved immensely because I was so proud of myself for pushing past my own boundaries, and I am so glad that this moment was captured by Charlotte.

Reflecting on these images takes me back to the clean mountain air, the scent of fall, and the echoes of our laughter ringing through the vast wilderness. Each photo shows a unique part of our journey the serenity of dawn, the pure joy of shared experiences, and the pride of overcoming challenges. Together, these moments make a map of our memories, reminding me that travel is not just about the places we visit or dream to go, but the memories we create and the connections we strengthen.
